ABOUT US
‍
ENKAY LLC is a boutique Management Consultancy and Financial Advisory company licensed by SHAMS FZ. ENKAY is a multi-disciplinary outfit and works with its Clients to bring  them business solutions that enable them to achieve their performance and growth objectives.  

We structure and execute capital and management solutions tailor made to the Client's requirement. We are sector agnostic due to our extensive capability of our wider network, however our work practice has been skewed towards Oil & Gas, Manufacturing, Trade Finance, Real Estate , Agri Commodities, Precious Metals and Hospitality.
‍
We attend to any need of the Client pertaining to technical management assistance and or financial advisory such as raising debt/equity, acquisition and/or divesting.
